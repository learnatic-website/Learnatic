Our Story

Learn how Learnatic began and our journey to becoming a leading online learning platform.

Transforming Education Through Innovation

Founded in 2018, Learnatic started with a simple mission: to make quality education accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our founders, experienced educators and tech professionals, recognized the growing need for flexible, skill-based learning in today's fast-paced digital world.

What began as a small platform with just 15 courses has grown into a global learning community serving over 300,000 students across 120+ countries. We've partnered with industry experts, universities, and companies to develop courses that are not only educational but also practical and job-ready.

Today, Learnatic continues to innovate

Our Mission

To democratize education by providing affordable, high-quality courses that equip learners with the skills needed to thrive in the 21st-century workforce. We aim to break down barriers to education and create opportunities for lifelong learning and career advancement.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Education should be available to everyone, regardless of location or background.
  • Quality: We maintain high standards in course content and instruction.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our platform and teaching methods.
  • Community: Building supportive learning environments where students can grow together.
  • Impact: Measuring success by the positive change in our students' lives.
